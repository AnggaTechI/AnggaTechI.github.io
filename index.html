About AnggaTechI

      

        This site is designed as a lightweight personal landing page for the AnggaTechI brand.
        It helps search engines and visitors understand who AnggaTechI is, what topics are being worked on,
        and where to find the main public projects. The content is intentionally clear, text-rich,
        and structured so it remains readable for both users and indexing systems.
